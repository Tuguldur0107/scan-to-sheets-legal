Scan to Sheets

Scan to Sheets is a mobile app that scans barcodes and appends results to a Google Spreadsheet selected by the user.

Privacy Policy

The app accesses Google account data only after the user signs in and grants consent. It lists spreadsheets (Drive metadata) and writes scan rows to the spreadsheet and tab the user selects (Google Sheets). The app does not sell personal data.
